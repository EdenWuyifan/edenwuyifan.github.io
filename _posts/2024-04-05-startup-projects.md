## üíº RichCRM

**AI-Powered Customer Relationship Management Platform for Professional Service Providers**  
[![GitHub Frontend](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Frontend-181717?logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/Richtech-AI-Lab/RichCRM-frontend) [![GitHub Backend](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Backend-181717?logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/Richtech-AI-Lab/RichCRM-backend)

### Overview

RichCRM is a specialized customer relationship management platform designed for real estate law firms and professional service providers. Unlike traditional CRMs that suffer from feature overload and lack customization, RichCRM integrates AI-powered automation, modular design, and streamlined case management to enhance efficiency and client transparency.

### Value Proposition

**For professional agency service entities** who need digital assistance on workflow and case management with fewer resources, **RichCRM** provides efficient and fluent case/communication management. Unlike traditional CRM systems, our product integrates various office tools and AI functions to save cases and work with users in the workflow.

### Key Features

- **AI-Powered Automation**: LangChain-based agent for intelligent task automation and workflow optimization
- **Case Management**: Detailed case tracking and client interaction management tailored for legal workflows
- **Document Management**: Integrated file management system with secure storage and organization
- **Client Communication**: Streamlined communication tools with client transparency features
- **Mobile Support**: Robust mobile accessibility for on-the-go professionals
- **Modular Customization**: Industry-specific modules that adapt to niche professional service needs
- **API Integration**: Seamless integration with office tools and third-party services

### Problem Statement

Traditional CRMs create significant frustrations for small and medium-sized law firms, including:
- **Feature overload** with steep learning curves
- **Inefficiency** from juggling multiple fragmented tools
- **Poor case management** lacking industry-specific customization
- **Lack of transparency** leading to client dissatisfaction

RichCRM addresses these challenges by providing affordable, AI-driven solutions specifically designed for professional service providers, particularly real estate law firms handling litigation cases.

### Market Position

**Target Market**: Real estate law firms and small-to-medium legal offices (5-50 employees) in urban markets like NYC, where:
- 49% growth in litigation cases
- 15,000+ active law firms and 130,000+ lawyers
- 400-600 daily housing-related cases

**Competitive Advantage**:
- **AI-driven automation** tailored to legal workflows (unlike generic competitors)
- **Affordable pricing** ($15-50/user/month) vs. expensive enterprise solutions
- **Industry-specific customization** vs. one-size-fits-all platforms
- **Mobile-first design** for modern professional workflows

### Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Modern web application framework
- **Backend**: Scalable server architecture with robust API design
- **AI/ML**: LangChain agent integration for intelligent automation
- **Database**: Secure, scalable data management
- **Security**: Advanced security measures for sensitive client data
- **DevOps**: Agile development with continuous integration/deployment

### Status & Traction

**Current Status**: Beta v0.0.7 - Successfully launched MVP1 with core functionalities:
- ‚úÖ Case management system
- ‚úÖ File management
- ‚úÖ Contact management
- ‚úÖ Client communication tools
- ‚úÖ LangChain AI agent integration

**Traction**:
- First client onboarded (also seed-round investor)
- Active beta testing and feedback collection
- Agile iteration through 7 versions
- Production-ready technology stack deployed

**E-commerce Platform for Original Artworks & Interior D√©cor**  
[![GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Repo-181717?logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/Artitone/ARTITONE)

### Overview

ARTITONE is an e-commerce marketplace specializing in original artworks and interior d√©cor pieces crafted by emerging artists and designers. We offer a wide range of categories including paintings, photographs, sculptures, prints, wall decor, vases, rugs, curtains, and small furniture pieces.

### Solution & Technology

We leverage AI algorithms and zero-shot models on our server to extract keywords from artwork images uploaded by artists. This innovation:
- **Shortens the artwork registration process** significantly
- **Enhances search and recommendation capabilities** through color palette and texture analysis
- **Improves user experience** with intelligent content discovery

### Problem Statement

Individuals seeking affordable decoration options often struggle to find quality pieces, while emerging artists lack visibility and platforms to showcase and sell their work. ARTITONE bridges this gap by connecting non-luxury buyers with talented young artists.

### Target Customers

As an online marketplace, we serve two primary customer segments:
- **Emerging artists** seeking visibility and sales channels
- **Art and decoration enthusiasts** who need help making informed purchasing decisions
